MISSION OVERVIEW

Our missions focus on preserving life and restoring security across high-risk situations Below are the primary mission profiles JSC Unit undertakes

Search and Rescue

Rapid response teams deploy to locate and extract civilians in disaster zones hostile environments or collapsed structures Emphasis on medical stabilization quick extraction and secure evacuation routes

Counter-Terrorism

Precision raids and containment strategies to neutralize active threats Team coordinates intelligence surveillance and surgical intervention to minimize collateral damage

Hostage Rescue

High-stakes breaching and close-quarters tactics focused on securing hostages safe extraction and suspect containment Dynamic entry planning and real-time negotiation support are core capabilities

Rules of Engagement

Use of force is governed by mission directives and legal oversight All missions prioritize non-lethal options and de-escalation when feasible but allow decisive force to protect innocent lives
